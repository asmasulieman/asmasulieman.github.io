Life journey

A little about my life; I was born in Mogadishu, Somalia aka "The horn of Africa."I don't remember much from my time in Somalia, becuase we migrated to India due to the war happening at the time. During my time in India, Hyderabad I learned three new languages while learning my mother tongue Somali. After sometime in India we migrated to the States. The first state we lived in was Kentucky, then moved around the midwest a bit and now I'm in Madison. My journey was not linear whatsoever. Graudating from highschool and being able to have the privilege to give the graduation speech was a stepping stone for me and a moment of growth.
